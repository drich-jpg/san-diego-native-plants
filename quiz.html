San Diego Native Plants

site and photos by David Richards

San Diego is a very diverse county and there are several diverse communities of plants that can be found across the county. Three of these plant communities are Coastal Sage Scrub (CSS), Chaparral, and Desert Scrub. How well do you know these plants? Find out with this interactive quiz! Still need to study first? Practice flipping through these interactive flashcards or browse through the plant catalog.
